## run_analysis.R
proj.init() Used to download and extract the data
proj.data() Processes the extracted data and creates tidy.txt

## Identifiers
* ===========================================================================
* Subject   = ID of the test subject
* Activity  = Activity performed with corresponding measurements
* ===========================================================================


## Activity Labels
* ===========================================================================
* Value | Label               | Description
* ===========================================================================
* 1     | WALKING             | Subject walking during the test
* 2     | WALKING_UPSTAIRS    | Subject walking up a staircase during the test
* 3     | WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS  | Subject walking down a staircase during the test
* 4     | SITTING             | Subject sitting during the test
* 5     | STANDING            | Subject standing during the test
* 6     | LAYING              | Subject laying down during the test
